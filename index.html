Research

My research focuses on advancing 3D imaging through next-generation sensor technologies and machine learning. Currently, I am developing methods to improve time-of-flight 3D imaging using single-photon avalanche diode (SPAD) cameras, aiming to make high-resolution depth sensing more accurate and efficient. In my undergrad, I developed supervised transformer vision models for the reconstruction of sinograms.
